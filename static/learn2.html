Learn Paritaire

Playing paritaire is simple enough to get you started in seconds, and yet complex enough to stay interesting for hours! You can play, pause and rejoin anytime, all without signup or login! Below, we show you how to start, play and share a game.

How do I start a game?

To start a new game session, click the New button in the navigation bar. This will lead you to the set up page, where you can configure the following parameters:

Settings

  • Field Size
    The field size is set to 8x8 by default, but you can choose a larger field if you want to.
    A larger field will make the game more complex, but it will also take longer time to finish.

  • Game Mode

  • Colors
    You can choose two of these beautiful colors for you and your opponent.

When you are satisfied with your choice, click the "Ready!" button. This will move you directly into the game.
Altough, if you have chosen online mode, you'll have to wait until an opponent joins your session.

What are the rules?

Paritaire is a game you might already know as reversi or othello: Your main goal is to have more tokens than your opponent when the game ends.

But how can I reach this goal?
Every game starts with four crossed tokens.
Both players can place their tokens alternately by following these two rules:

  1. You can only place your tokens next to an opposing token (horizontally, vertically or diagonally).
  2. You can only put it there, if the new one and one of your other tokens will flank
    at least one opposing token in a straight line (horizontally, vertically or diagonally).
After placing a token, all the opposing tokens that get flanked by your move will change their color to become yours. If a player can not place any tokens, the other player can turn again.

When does the game end?
The game ends when there is no possibility left to place any token. The player with most tokens at this point wins the game.

What game modes are available?

When setting up a game, you can choose between different game modes. At the moment, you can choose between online and local mode:

  • Online
    Choose this mode, if you and your opponent both want to use their own device (smartphone, tablet or computer).
    After you have created a session, you have to wait until your opponent joins your session. Learn here how to join others sessions.

  • Local
    Choose this mode, if you want to play on a single device. In local mode, you and your opponent should sit next to each other, so that both can place their tokens alterningly on the same screen.

How do I share and join games?

In general, you can join a game by opening it's session link in a browser.

How do I get a session link?
There are three drifferent situations:

  • If you already have an opponent, but no session, you can start a new session in online mode.

  • If your opponent already created a session, ask him to send you the session link via e-mail, instant messenger, etc.
    He can get that link from his browsers address bar or by pressing the "Share" button on the game page.

  • If you don't have an opponent yet, you can publish your own session with the "Publish" button, or join a public session by selecting one in the public list.
    You can find that list by clicking the "Public" button in the navigation bar, or on the start page. If you publish a session, it will be available in this list until it expires (after 1h) or an opponent joins.
